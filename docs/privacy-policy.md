# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-19

## What data is stored
The app stores the following data on your device:
- Name/profile inputs
- Targets and planner scores
- Habits and completion history
- Weekly/monthly review inputs
- Notification preferences

## Where data is stored
Data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

## What we do not do
- We do not collect personal app-entry data on remote servers.
- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not share personal data with third parties.
- We do not run analytics on your local entries.

## Purchases
All features are currently included in the free v1 release. If paid features return later, purchases are processed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not store payment card information.
